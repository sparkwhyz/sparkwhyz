Sponsor a Classroom

Help put prevention education into students’ hands.

Classroom sponsorships help provide SparkWhyz books, educational materials, and student-led safety programming.

Why Sponsor?

One classroom can influence an entire community.

A sponsored classroom can receive copies of the SparkWhyz children’s book, discussion materials, and opportunities to participate in student-led fire-prevention education.

Sponsors help make the program accessible to schools and families without placing the full cost on teachers or school budgets.

Final sponsorship levels will be confirmed after printing, classroom-set, and distribution costs are finalized.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about getting involved.

Who can participate in SparkWhyz?

Students, families, teachers, schools, firefighters, businesses, and community organizations can all participate.

Can students outside Laguna Beach participate?

Yes. SparkWhyz began in Laguna Beach, but students from other schools and communities can express interest in starting a chapter.

Does it cost money to join a chapter?

SparkWhyz plans to keep student participation accessible. Any future chapter costs or fundraising expectations will be explained before students commit.

Will website forms open Google Forms?

No. Forms will submit directly from SparkWhyz.org to Google Sheets. Visitors will remain on the website and receive an on-page confirmation.

How can a school invite SparkWhyz?

Teachers, administrators, parent organizations, and student leaders can contact SparkWhyz to discuss presentations, books, chapters, and classroom partnerships.
