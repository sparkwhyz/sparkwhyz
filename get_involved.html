The SparkWhyz Pledge

I pledge not to buy, possess, light, or encourage the use of illegal fireworks. I will make safe choices, speak up when something is dangerous, and help protect people, animals, homes, firefighters, and the natural environment.

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about getting involved.

Who can participate in SparkWhyz?

Students, parents, teachers, schools, firefighters, businesses, and community organizations can all participate.

Does it cost money to join a chapter?

SparkWhyz plans to make student participation accessible. Any future chapter costs or fundraising expectations will be explained clearly before students commit.

Can a student start a chapter outside Laguna Beach?

Yes. SparkWhyz is beginning in Laguna Beach, but students from other schools and communities can express interest in starting a chapter.

How will form information be collected?

Website forms will submit information directly to secure Google Sheets. Visitors will remain on SparkWhyz.org and will not be redirected to a Google Form.

How can a school invite SparkWhyz?

Teachers, administrators, parent organizations, and student leaders can contact SparkWhyz to discuss presentations, books, chapters, or classroom partnerships.
