Prevention Through Partnership

Real fire-safety knowledge makes the message stronger.

SparkWhyz was created to prevent dangerous decisions before they lead to a wildfire, injury, or emergency.

Working with the Laguna Beach Fire Department helps connect our youth-led outreach with experienced fire-safety professionals who understand the risks facing our local community.

Together, we can help students understand why illegal fireworks are dangerous, how quickly fires can spread, and why prevention matters in a community surrounded by dry hillsides and open space.

How We Work Together

Connecting education with real community safety.

SparkWhyz combines student leadership with trusted guidance from local fire-safety professionals.
