    <section>

      <h2>About SparkWhyz</h2>

      <p>Inspired by the recent wildfire in Laguna Beach and modeled after other youth-led environmental campaigns, SparkWhyz was founded by local Laguna Beach School District teens Dastan (15) and Rosteen (13). Their goal is to create a cultural shift among youth, making fire safety a visible and peer-driven priority. By turning in their own fireworks to the Laguna Beach Fire Department, they hope to set a powerful example for others across the city and beyond.</p>

    </section>

    <section>

      <h2>How to Get Involved</h2>
