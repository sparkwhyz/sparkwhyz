Why Lead a Chapter?

Turn awareness into action.

SparkWhyz chapter leaders help students understand the risks of illegal fireworks and the importance of preventing fires before they start.

Chapters can organize pledge campaigns, school presentations, book outreach, community events, social media projects, and service activities.

Student leaders receive guidance, project ideas, educational materials, and support from the SparkWhyz team.

What Chapter Leaders Do

Lead projects that make a real difference.

Each chapter can adapt its activities to fit its school, age group, and community.

Lead Students

Organize meetings, assign roles, welcome volunteers, and help students work together toward shared goals.

Educate Peers

Share age-appropriate fire-prevention messages through presentations, campaigns, books, posters, and social media.

Build Partnerships

Work with teachers, parents, firefighters, student groups, businesses, and community organizations.

What Happens Next?

We will help you take the first steps.

Once your interest form is submitted, the SparkWhyz team can review your school, grade level, and goals.

Students may need a teacher or school staff advisor, depending on their school's club requirements.

SparkWhyz can provide suggested roles, meeting ideas, event plans, educational materials, and support for launching the chapter.
