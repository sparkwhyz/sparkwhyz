About SparkWhyz

Young people protecting the places they call home.

SparkWhyz is a youth-led fire-prevention initiative created to educate students, inspire safer decisions, and stop fireworks-related wildfires before they start.

Our Story

A close call became a community mission.

SparkWhyz began after a wildfire was started in Laguna Beach by a middle-school-aged boy using illegal fireworks.

Instead of focusing only on blame, our family asked a different question: what if someone had reached that student before he made the decision?

We realized that young people often hear warnings after something dangerous happens. SparkWhyz was created to reach students earlier, while there is still time to prevent the next fire.

What began as one family's response has grown into a youth-led effort involving students, families, firefighters, schools, and community leaders.

Our Mission

Prevention starts before the spark.

SparkWhyz gives students the knowledge, confidence, and opportunities they need to protect their schools, neighborhoods, families, and natural environment.

Prevent Wildfires

Teach students about the real risks of illegal fireworks before one impulsive decision becomes a devastating fire.

Empower Students

Give young people meaningful leadership roles so they can educate their peers and create positive change in their own communities.

Unite the Community

Bring together firefighters, schools, parents, students, businesses, and local organizations around one shared prevention mission.
